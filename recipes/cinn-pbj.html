Cinnamon Sugar Toast PB&J

Description

Standard peanut butter & jelly sandwich with cinnamon-sugar bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp raspberry jam

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine granulated sugar and ground cinnamon.
  2. Toast the bread.
  3. Butter one side of each of the bread slices and sprinkle generously with cinnamon sugar mixture.
  4. Spread peanut butter and jelly on the inside of each of the bread slices.
  5. Make a sandwich and cut in half. Serve immediately.
