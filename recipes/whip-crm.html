Whipped Cream

Description

Smooth, sweet, and fluffy. Add to fruit, coffee, or desserts.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Pour heavy cream and vanilla extract in a large glass bowl.
  2. Whip the cream and extract using an electric mixer until ingredients are fluffy and thick.
  3. Be careful not to overmix.
  4. Serve immediately.
