Whipped Cream

Description

Simple whipped cream. Add to fruit, coffee, or desserts.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 to 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Pour heavy cream and vanilla extract in a large, cold glass bowl.
  2. Whip the cream and extract using an electric mixer until ingredients are fluffy and thick.
  3. Be careful not to overmix.
  4. Serve immediately or refrigerate to use later.
